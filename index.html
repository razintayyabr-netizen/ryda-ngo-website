About RYDA

A civil society organization rooted in human dignity, justice, and equality.

Rohingya Youth Development Association is dedicated to strengthening the resilience of the Rohingya community by investing in education, leadership, human rights awareness, and sustainable community development.

RYDA works with local communities, civil society groups, and international partners to raise refugee voices, expand opportunities, and advocate for inclusive long-term solutions.

Mission

To promote justice, peace, and dignity for the Rohingya community by empowering youth, girls, and women through education, leadership development, and human rights advocacy, while representing refugee voices internationally, strengthening community resilience, and advocating for fundamental rights, protection, and a safe, voluntary return with citizenship and security.

RYDA also provides essential humanitarian assistance including food, shelter, healthcare, and education in refugee camps, while supporting skills training, education programs, and livelihoods that foster self-reliance.

Vision

A future where all Rohingya people are empowered and educated, contributing to a peaceful, just, and inclusive society where dignity, equality, and human rights are fully respected.

Why This Matters

Empowered youth are the foundation of a more resilient future.

By investing in leadership, education, and community initiatives, RYDA nurtures a generation prepared to create positive change, strengthen social cohesion, and build a more just and hopeful future for Rohingya communities.
