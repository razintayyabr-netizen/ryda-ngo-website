Editorial Guidelines

Standards for accurate, ethical, and impactful reporting on Rohingya issues.

📌 Post Categories

  • Statement — Official RYDA positions and public statements on events or incidents.
  • Report — Documentation briefs, human rights reports, field reports.
  • Emergency — Urgent updates about humanitarian crises, floods, fires, or displacement.
  • Advocacy — Submissions to UN bodies, campaign updates, international engagement.
  • Leadership — Youth training, community leadership programs, capacity building updates.
  • Research — Research papers, surveys, evidence-based analysis and publications.
  • Documentation — Human rights monitoring updates, violation documentation reports.

✍️ Writing Standards

  • Write in clear, plain English accessible to international readers.
  • Use specific dates, locations, and numbers where available.
  • Do not identify victims or survivors by name without explicit consent.
  • Attribute quotes clearly: "According to RYDA documentation…" or "Community members reported…"
  • Separate facts from analysis — clearly label opinion or advocacy positions.
  • Use "Rohingya" (not Rohingyan or Bengali) — the community's preferred self-identification.

🛡️ Safety & Verification

  • Verify all claims with at least two independent sources before publishing.
  • Never publish identifying information that could endanger individuals in Myanmar or camps.
  • Review field reports for operational security before publishing locations of active work.
  • All emergency updates must be reviewed by RYDA leadership before publishing.

📷 Images

  • Only use images you have the rights to publish.
  • Do not publish images that identify individuals without consent.
  • Recommended hosts: Cloudinary (free tier), Imgur, or your own storage.
  • Image URL must be a direct link ending in .jpg, .png, .webp, or .gif.

🔑 Access & Security

  • Never share your writer token with unauthorized people.
  • If you believe the token has been compromised, change the WRITER_TOKEN env variable in Vercel immediately.
  • Always log out when using a shared or public computer.
